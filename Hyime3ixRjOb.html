A brief, pithy, serviceable survey of English life on the eve of the Norman Conquest. A non-scholarly, very easy to read and enjoyable book. Not as detailed as I'd like it (I would be interested in more day-to-day historical details) it nevertheless is a real page-turned for in-between 'heavier' books. This is a stunningly fun read given the topic.

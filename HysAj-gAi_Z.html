More About Fantastic Four, Volume 3

This is volume is a big step up from the first two volumes. I found the first two volumes confusing and loaded with information, that (at the time) felt like it had no purpose. I was glad to see a lot of the threads from the previous volumes getting picked up again and the different storylines coming together. I particularly enjoyed... Reed goes full Whiplash while giving a TED talk. Shuts down his version of TED and creates a new one with blackjack and hook... I mean with people young enough to not be jaded. His speech was sassy and had some great lines. I also enjoyed Future Franklin and Valeria's adventures. Reed, Ben, and Doom from the past rang a little hollow... I think these have to be read all together. It's been a few months since I read volume 2. Some of the previous plot lines were a little fuzzy. Overall, good stories.

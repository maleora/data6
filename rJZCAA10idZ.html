More About Spacehawk

Brilliant moment in comic history turned tragic when a prolific, febrile imagination is forced to write war propaganda. Great collection of gorgeous (insofar as the grotesque can be gorgeous) Wolverton comics. The early stories are the most interesting, before Spacehawk takes off his mask and morphs into basically a standard-issue superhero. In the early stories, his mask and the mystery surrounding him make him nearly as creepy as the villains. And... The first half of the book (the space stuff) is five fucking stars. After that, where the editors step in and tell Wolverton to drop what he's doing and focus on the war, well.. the comic just starts to suck. For example, Spacehawk goes from fighting cosmic beings to tire thieves. So, yeah.
